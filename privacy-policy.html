Security We endeavor to safeguard and protect our Users’ personal information. All of our Users’ personal information is restricted in our offices, as well as the offices of our third-party service providers. Any transmission of data at or through the Site, other SHIFT44™ Offerings or otherwise via the Internet or wireless networks, is done at your own risk.
